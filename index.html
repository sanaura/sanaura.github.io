MY MISSION

To pursue a career in urban planning

My short-term goal is to do a master's degree program on urban planning with a scholarship. Afterwards, I would like to do research on this topic or enter companies related to it.

I received a bachelor's degree in architecture a few years ago. During my studies, I became interested in urban planning. It is a discipline closely related to people's well-being, from designing public parks for families to enjoy their weekends to creating safe routes for cyclists. The environment we design is aimed at every group in society, regardless of their financial status. These contemplations motivated me to forge a career in this field of study.

In the following sections, I am happy to share the projects that I completed during my studies.

PROJECT #1

Adaptive Architecture in Former Mining Areas

The distinct characteristic of the proposal was to take advantage of the changing tides. During low tide, the open area can be used as a skatepark; whereas when the tide is high, people can sit on the stairs while relaxing.

PROJECT #3

Culinary centre at Gunung Anyar

This was a proposal to restore an aging culinary centre at Gunung Anyar, Surabaya. The playground area is for children to play while waiting for the meals to be prepared. Grafities created by local artist add to the unique features of the place. The wooden floor is designed in such a way to make it reconfigurable.
